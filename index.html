Why Ethernet?

We have shit internet. I cannot watch shows or play LEAGUE. From testing, we have 20-30 mbps! This is unusuable even for academic purposes. From testing, I attained 150 mbps on my MacBook (very good). This is an almost 7 times increase! Ethernet transforms our internet into a haven for academics, seamless online socializing, and the ultimate Netflix experience, turning academic frustration into digital beauty.

Won't this slow our wifi?

This is not true at all. Ethernet is a wired connection, so it will not affect your wifi at all. In fact, it will make your wifi faster because it will take some of the load off of your wifi. This causes reduced Wi-Fi congestion, lower latency, more consistent performance and better overall network performance.

Can I myself get the Ethernet?

Absolutely! There is 4 ethernet slots. You can buy an ethernet cable from Amazon or Best Buy. You can also buy an ethernet adapter for your laptop. If you have a Macbook, you can buy a USB-C to ethernet adapter. If you have a Windows laptop, you can buy a USB to ethernet adapter. I can help you set it up.

Won't this Ethernet cable look horrible?

This is a fair argument but there are ways to make it look better. What I plan to do is to buy a 50 foot ethernet cable and run it along the wall. I will then tape it to the wall and run it along the ceiling. I will then tape it to the ceiling and run it to my desk. This will make it look much better.

Could this be a safety or communical concern?

No! Ethernet has never caused harm to anyone unless the cable is on the floor and someone trips on it. This is why I recommend running it along the wall and ceiling. This will make it look better and safer. As for Communial, unless Ethernet is considered furniture, it is not a communial concern.
